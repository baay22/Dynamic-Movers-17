The most dog-friendly places in NYC

In NYC there are a lot of restaurants, bars, hotels, and off-leash parks where your four-legged friend can enjoy. If you are moving to NYC check out some of the most dog-friendly places there.

  • The 79th Street Boat Basin – This is one of the most dog-friendly restaurants in NYC. It is opened from April to October. It is completely open-air, you can take your pup anywhere, including the restrooms.

  • Barking Dog – It is not only pet friendly but also has a pup-centric name. It has ample sidewalk seating and doggy water fountains.

  • Shake Shack – It offers a doggie menu, which includes the Pooch-ini (dog biscuits, peanut butter sauce, and vanilla custard), and Bag O’Bones (dog biscuits from Bocce’s Bakery). Your dog will be happy here and this is one of the most dog-friendly places in NYC.

  • The Park – It is a restaurant in West Chelsea. Your dog can join you at any of the outside tables in the garden when you dine and drink.

If you are just visiting NYC, there are also a lot of hotels that are dog-friendly. One of them is the Muse Hotel which offers an exclusive welcome package for dogs when they check-in, and dog walking and pet sitting services are available at an additional charge. In NYC there are a lot of places that are dog-friendly.

Make your dog feel happy.

Besides this, there is one place that will completely spoil your dog. It is a Downtown Doghouse – dog spa and salon. You can opt for a nail trim or hydro surge massage and spoil your four-legged friend.

These are some of the most dog-friendly places in NYC. When you are moving with a dog it is also stressful for him so make sure you find some places that are dog-friendly and that will make your pet feel comfortable and happy.
